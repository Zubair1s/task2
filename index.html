From roof survey to flipping the switch

Four steps, start to finish. Most homes are generating power within 6–8 weeks of signing.

  1. 01

    Roof & usage assessment

    We pull satellite roof data and 12 months of your utility bills to model real production, not a guess.

  2. 02

    Custom system design

    An engineer lays out panel placement, wiring and inverter choice for your specific roof shape and shading.

  3. 03

    Permits & installation

    We handle permitting and utility paperwork. Physical install typically takes one to two days on-site.

  4. 04

    Inspection & power-on

    Once your utility signs off, we switch the system on and walk you through the monitoring app.
